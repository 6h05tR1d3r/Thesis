Fake News Detector

Introduction

This is the webpage regarding the fake news article detection. Each algortihm used has it's own page. Models are pre-trained on the datasets extracted from the internet. On each page you can find the link to the dataset used for training and some information about the model and some viusal representation of the data. From the information provided you can decide which model you want to use to aid you in the decisin making.

DISCLAIMER: THE MODELS ARE NOT 100% ACCURATE.

  • Model 1: Logistic Regression
  • Model 2: Naive Bayes
  • Model 3: Passive Agressive Classifier

How to use:

Each Model has it's own webpage. They will be shown on the sidebar on the left.

On each page you can find the link to the dataset used for training and some information about the model and some viusal representation of the data. From the information provided you can decide which model you want to use to aid you in the decisin making.

The first page is dedicated to the Combined models, the second one is dedicated to Logistic Regressin and the third to Naive Bayes model.

At the bottom part of the page you can use buttons to generate graphs which display ratio of Real and Fake news.

Also at the bottom you can generate wordcloud, which will display the most common words in the dataset.

PAY ATTENTION: WHEN GENERATING GRAPHS YOU WILL LOSE YOUR RESULT FROM THE MODELS.
