About the model:

Logistic regression is a supervised machine learning model used for classification of data.

This specific iteration of the model is best used for smaller news articles between 3500 and 4000 characters, preferably about politics, and specifically about Donald Trump since the training data was focused on this topic.

The submitted data is not stored anywhere; it is only preprocessed, and the output is displayed to the user. Refreshing the page will clear the data.

The model is Logistic Regression, trained on vectorized data for research purposes, which may limit its application.
